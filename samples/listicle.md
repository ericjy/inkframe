# Why Time is More Important Than Money?

### ***5 key reasons you must know***
1. **Finite Time**. We all have limited lifespan.

2. **Non-Renewable**. Unlike money, time can't be regained. Once spent, it's gone forever.

3. **Equal Playing Field**. Everyone has the same 24 hours, no matter their wealth or status.

4. **Foundation**. Developing skills career, health, or relationship, all require time.

5. **Opportunity Cost**. Wasting time means missing opportunities to earn.
